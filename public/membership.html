How to Join

4 Simple Steps

1

Visit IEEE.org

2

Create Account

Register with your DBATU email and fill in personal details.

3

Select Membership

Choose "Student Member" and find our chapter (DBATU, Lonere).

4

Pay & Join

Complete payment and receive your IEEE Member ID. Welcome!
