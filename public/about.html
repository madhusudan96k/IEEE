Introduction

What is IEEE?

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) is the world's largest technical professional organisation dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. Founded in 1963, it unites engineers and scientists across 160+ countries.

IEEE publishes 30% of the world's technical literature in electrical engineering, electronics, and computer science, and has developed over 1,300 active industry standards — including Wi-Fi (IEEE 802.11) and Ethernet (IEEE 802.3).

Our Student Chapter at DBATU, Lonere brings this global network right to campus — fostering technical skills, leadership, and a community of innovators since 2010.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯 Mission

To foster technological innovation and excellence, provide a platform for DBATU student engineers to develop professional skills, and connect members to the global IEEE community through events, workshops, and collaborative projects.

🔭 Vision

To be the most impactful IEEE Student Chapter in Maharashtra — producing technically excellent, socially responsible, and globally competitive engineers who advance technology for humanity.

📅 Our History at DBATU

Established in 2010 by passionate engineering students at Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, our branch has grown from 30 members to 350+ active members, hosting 80+ events, winning regional and national awards, and publishing IEEE papers.

🌍 Global IEEE Presence

As part of IEEE Bombay Section and the global IEEE network, our members get access to conferences, journals, certifications, internship listings, and connections spanning 160+ countries and 400,000+ members worldwide.
